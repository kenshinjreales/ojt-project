STUDENT LICENSE QUALIFICATIONS


For Filipino citizens, you must be at least 16 years old to qualify for a student license.

Physically and mentally fit to operate a vehicle.

Be able to read and write in Filipino or English.

Have finished the LTO's 15-hour Online Theoretical Driving Course.

No unsettled traffic violations.

Must have a valid email address.


NOTE: If you are a foreign national, you must be at least 18 years of age and living in the Philippines for at least one year,

with proof of stay for at least six months.


---------------------------------------------------------




STUDENT PERMIT REQUIREMENTS


A fully accomplished Application for Driver's License (ADL).

Original and photocopy of Philippine Statistics Authority (PSA) birth certificate.

Original medical certificate e-transmitted from any LTO-accredited medical facility and/or physician.

If you are a married woman, prepare a marriage certificate issued by PSA.

One original copy and one photocopy of any government-issued ID with a photo and signature.

The 15-hr Theoretical Driving Course certificate from an LTO-accredited driving school.

Taxpayer’s Identification Number (TIN), if employed.


NOTE: Please keep in mind that a student permit has a one-year validity from the date of issue

and you can only drive a car or motorcycle if a licensed driver is present.
