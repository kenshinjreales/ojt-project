Who Can Obtain a 10-year license?


Drivers with a clean driving record can apply for a 10-year valid license.

This only means that the driver should have NO DEMERITS on their driving record.


---------------------------------------------------------




REQUIREMENTS

1. Driver's License Card (Professional or Non-Professional)

2. Medical Certificate (must be issued by a facility/physician that is LTO-accredited)

3. Comprehensive Driver's Education Certificate (CDE)

4. Fully Accomplished Application Form for Driver's License
