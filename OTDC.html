ONLINE THEORETICAL DRIVING COURSE


This 15hr-course has been required since 2020 in order to acquire a student permit here in the Philippines.

This was LTO's solution to fix and resolve traffic issues here in the Philippines.

This course will also give the students a better understanding on how to stay safe on the road, develop a better driving attitude,

better understanding of road signs, and develop a clearer driver intuition.


At the end of the course, a completion certificate will be given to the student.
